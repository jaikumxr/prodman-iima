About the Club

We build product leaders at IIM Ahmedabad, from workshops and case competitions to placements, publications, and a community that stays curious.

Who We Are

The ProdMan club is the Product Management Club of IIM Ahmedabad — a student-run community built for everyone who believes great products can change the world. We exist to help students explore, understand, and build careers in product management.

Through curated events, industry speaker sessions, peer-learning initiatives, and a strong alumni network, The ProdMan club bridges the gap between IIMA's rigorous academic environment and the skills expected by the world of product roles at global technology companies and top startups.

What We Do

  • Publish product teardowns, prep books, and a flagship magazine authored by the IIMA product community
  • Help students land their dream PM roles and internships through mock interviews, CV reviews, and provide guidance through buddy programs
  • Build product sense, critical thinking, and technical skills through workshops and competitions
  • Host cross-campus case competitions, speaker series, and hands-on product challenges year-round

Our Cells

Five specialized cells, each driving a distinct student outcome.

📰

Publications Cell

Creates content that is accurate, clear, and impactful — Product Mindset prep book, ProdMag magazine, product teardown series, and the SIP experience compendium.

💼

Placements Cell

Ensures every PGP1, PGP2, and PGPX aspirant is interview-ready — 50+ mock interviews, 50+ CV reviews for SIP, buddy programs, and an alumni-led prep series.

🏅

Events & Community Cell

Gives the IIMA community opportunities to showcase and hone PM skills through case competitions, quizzes, and cross-campus events like Adobe Sprint and Proxima.

🎤

Speakers & Workshops Cell

Connects the IIMA community with industry-leading experts — year-round speaker series, PM-101 sessions, hands-on workshops, and closed-room discussions for product builders.

🎨

Design Cell

Creates the visual identity for all ProdMan events and publications — event posters, social media content, magazine layouts, and the Product Mindset redesign.
